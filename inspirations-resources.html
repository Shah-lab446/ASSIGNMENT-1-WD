Inspirations & Resources

Where I Get Inspired

  • ArtStation: Check out portfolios from some of the best 3D artists and animators out there. Visit ArtStation
  • Pixar Animation Studios: Watch behind-the-scenes videos and learn from the pros. Visit Pixar
  • CGSociety: Join a community of digital artists sharing cool stuff and tutorials. Visit CGSociety
  • YouTube Channels: Channels like Blender Guru, Gleb Alexandrov, and FlippedNormals have awesome tutorials and inspiration.

Helpful Resources

  • Blender: A free and open-source 3D creation tool that's great for beginners. Download Blender
  • Autodesk Maya: The industry standard for animation and modeling. Learn about Maya
  • Unreal Engine: A powerful tool for real-time 3D creation used in games and visualization. Explore Unreal Engine
  • Udemy & Coursera: Online platforms with courses on 3D design and animation, from basics to advanced stuff.
  • Poly Haven: Free high-quality 3D assets and HDRIs you can use in your projects. Visit Poly Haven
